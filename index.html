About

I am a PhD researcher working in photonics, plasmonics, and quantum optical sensing. My research focuses on developing high-sensitivity optical sensing systems using Surface-Enhanced Raman Spectroscopy (SERS), optical interferometry, and computational analysis.

My goal is to build next-generation sensing platforms for biomedical diagnostics and precision optical measurement by integrating experimental photonics and intelligent data analysis.

Research Interests

  • Surface-Enhanced Raman Scattering (SERS)
  • Photonics and plasmonics
  • Optical spectroscopy
  • Machine learning for sensing
  • Quantum optical sensing
